Important Notes
The annual Judge’s conference is set for Dec. 3-5, 2025. Some courtrooms will be closed during that time, and the HUB bail system will be in place. The HUB bail details are being finalized and will be communicated at a later date. Given the timeline we wanted to provide some direction for UTA dates for files coming to 222 Main St. Vancouver/DCC or Robson Square. For all locations, please instruct your members to not issue UTA’s for Dec. 3, 4, or 5, 2025. For 222 Main Street Vancouver files please also avoid Dec. 29, 20, 31 and Jan. 2nd. DCC will be open those days. Your Robson Square/Vancouver youth court or circuit court matters the typical days fall on Christmas Day and New's Years Day which should also be avoided as the courts will be closed those days.

Update - October 18, 2025: The dates for the court conference (Dec. 3-5, 2025) and end of December/early January (Dec. 29, 30, 31 and Jan. 2) have been blocked off in this calculator. However, please still double-check all information to ensure accuracy.

Update - November 21, 2025: Fingerprint scheduling has been updated. Prints are now scheduled for 1 business day prior to court (previously 2 days). Prints can now occur Monday through Friday (previously Tuesday-Thursday only). Times remain 0900hrs for A/C squads and 1800hrs for B/D squads.
